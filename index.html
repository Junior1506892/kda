About KDA School

Founded in 1995, KDA School has been dedicated to nurturing young minds through a blend of academics, arts, and athletics. Our dedicated faculty and vibrant student community create an environment where every student can thrive and discover their passions. We are committed to preparing leaders of tomorrow with the values of integrity, respect, and innovation.
